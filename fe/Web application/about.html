Welcome to SPACEBIRDS

Put the world of satellites in your fingerprints! SPACEBIRDS is a powerful tool to learn about satellites and the grounds stations in which they communicate. Provides basic information such as name, type, orbit and much more.

Payloads

Rockets

Debris

Provides accurate visualization of the locations of the rockets in orbit.

Low Earth Orbit (LEO)

LEO shows all the payloads, rockets and debris found in the low Earth orbit. (More Information)

Medium Earth Orbit (MEO)

MEO shows all the payloads, rockets and debris found in the middle Earth layer. (More Information)

High Earth Orbit (HEO)

HEO shows all the payloads, rockets and debris found in the high Earth layer. (More Information)

ALL

Provides accurate visualization of the locations of the payloads, rockets and debris found in orbit.

Ground Stations (GS)

Provides visualization of the location of the ground stations.

Team

World wide collaboration for the development of new applications.

This team is composed of NASA World Wind Interns from around the World. Their primary goal was to develop an application with NASA Web World Wind platform which allows the visualization of the real-time location of space payloads, rockets, and debris, at Earth orbits.
